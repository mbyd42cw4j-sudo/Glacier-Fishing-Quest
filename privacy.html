Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

Our Commitment: Glacier Fishing Quest is built with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information, such as your name, email address, or device identifier. The game functions entirely locally on your device.

2. Information Storage

Since no data is collected, no data is stored on our servers. Any game progress is stored only on your local device.

3. Third Parties

We do not share any information with third parties. There are no analytics or advertising SDKs in the current version of the app that track user behavior.

If you have any questions, contact us at omabey@ukr.net.
