The Developer's Journey

My name is Dmytro Vitrozhar, and I am an aspiring independent developer. Glacier Fishing Quest is more than just a game to me; it is a result of immense effort, learning, and passion for creating something meaningful.

Every element of this game was built with the goal of bringing value and entertainment to the players. I am constantly working to improve the mechanics and the visual experience to ensure a high-quality product.

Currently, the app does not contain any paid features, advertisements, or subscriptions. I want every user to enjoy the full content for free as I establish my presence in the App Store.

Looking Forward

In the future, to support my journey as a developer and continue creating even better games, I plan to introduce premium features and subscription models. This will allow me to invest more time and resources into delivering even more value to you.
